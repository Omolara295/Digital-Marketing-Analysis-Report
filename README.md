## SUMMARY OF THE REPORT 
The report has track the different activities that were carried out during the last marketing campaign that was carried out on five (5) various digital channels, 
Google Ads, Instagram Ads, Email Ads, Referral and Influencers. It has also focus on key metrics like Ad spend, Impressions, Clicks, Conversions, 
Revenue and to measure ROI by products and category.
It also tracks the top 11 products that generated the highest revenue as well as the trend based by Ad spend by ROI and Clicks by Conversions.

## CONCLUSIONS 
The dataset analyzed in this report shows a clear strategy for our future marketing 
campaign.by applying the power of the Power BI Dashboard we have been able to

## RECOMMENDATION

Strategic Ad spend Optimization
Based on the point noted in the Ad spend where more money was invested in the Google Ads and it did not even give the highest ROI, 
therefore, this kind of issue should be addressed in the coming campaign so that money invested can yield more ROI, also, products that are not doing well in terms should be focused on.

Also, to improve our engagement, more creative designs should be used in the content we put out for the Ads.

Also, in the coming month and year, more digital platforms should be considered for Ads during campaigns for example TikTok, 
Facebook and YouTube and X as these are some of the most used media platforms and more audience will be reached.
